The problem

The bottleneck isn’t your talent.
It’s the time you waste on forms.

The job search is a volume game. But most of your time goes to re-entering the same information into different portals — not to the work that actually gets you hired.

10–15m

Average time spent per application — done by hand

50–150

Applications the average candidate sends before landing a role

~40 hrs

Of your life spent doing nothing but filling out identical boxes

Repetitive by design

Every portal asks the same questions. Name, experience, education, LinkedIn URL — typed out again and again into different boxes on different sites.

The process rewards patience, not talent

ATS systems were built for recruiters, not applicants. The more patient you are with the grind, the more applications you send. That’s a broken system.

What it does

Everything you need to apply faster.

Open a job page. Let Apply do the heavy lifting. Review the answers. Apply — in minutes, not an hour.

Fills out every field for you, instantly

Apply detects every input on the page — text fields, dropdowns, checkboxes — and fills them based on your resume and work history. No copy-pasting, no re-typing.

  • Works across all major job portals including Greenhouse, Workday, Lever & Ashby
  • Answers are tailored to each job description, not just copy-pasted
  • You review and edit everything before submitting — you’re always in control

Chat with your resume using AI

Ask questions about your background and get instant career advice. Apply uses your resume to give tailored answers — powered by local AI, so your data never leaves your device.

  • Get personalized answers about your skills, experience, and strengths
  • Generate cover letters and interview prep in your voice
  • 100% private — everything runs locally through Ollama

Track every application in one place

Never lose track of where you’ve applied. Apply automatically logs each application — company, role, date, status — so you have a clear picture of your job search.

  • Automatically saved when you apply — no manual logging
  • See all your applications at a glance
  • Stored locally on your device, completely private

Find jobs that match your skills

Apply scores every job against your resume so you can focus on roles where you’re a strong fit — instead of spraying and praying.

  • AI-powered compatibility scores show how well you match each role
  • Filter by salary, location, remote, and job type
  • Apply directly from the results — no tab switching

How it works

Four steps. Minutes, not hours.

No setup wizard. No monthly subscription. Install the extension once and it just works whenever you open a job application.

01

Open a job page

Navigate to any job listing on any major platform. Apply wakes up automatically.

02

Extension reads the form

Apply scans the page, identifies every input field, and understands the context of the role.

03

AI fills it out

Your local AI generates tailored answers using your resume — in seconds, with no internet request.

Powered by Ollama
04

Review & apply

Read through the answers, make any tweaks you want, and click submit. Done.

Privacy first

Your data never leaves your laptop.

Most AI tools for job seekers send your resume, employment history, and personal details to their servers. Apply works completely differently.

  • The AI model runs entirely on your machine — no API calls, no cloud processing
  • Your resume and personal info is stored locally, never uploaded to any server
  • No account required — there’s nothing to sign in to, no data to collect
  • Fully open source — audit every line of code on GitHub
  • Free forever — no freemium, no subscription, no hidden costs
  • No cookies, analytics, fingerprinting, or tracking of any kind
  • All network requests go to localhost (Ollama) — zero external calls
